Shopping List Group Project

Here I am. My first group project. Nervous. Excited. Confused. Somewhat optimistic about the whole process. This was going to be the moment that you were going to be tested on a lot of the topics that we've covered in the past. Addding in another element, teamwork.

We've all had good and bad experiences when it comes to group projects in the past. Some team members are quiet, some loud. Some are hard working, some nothing. However, not to get all poetic with you, but this is where chemistry is made with people. You work with what you got and you move on. My partners Kris and Mara were pleasant to be around.

Kris was a monster machine. Making sure no one could break our code. And I mean no one. Well, I think his daughter did break it once..?

Mara, the logical killer. The brains of the operations. Their was not one syntax error she couldn't solve.

Me? What did I do? I took the glory and ran with it.
